# 📊 Sales Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents a **Sales Analysis Dashboard** built using Power BI to monitor sales performance, order volume, and product trends.

The dashboard provides insights into monthly sales trends, regional performance, and top-selling products, helping businesses make informed decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Track total sales and order volume  
- Analyze sales trends over time (monthly performance)  
- Identify top-performing product categories  
- Evaluate regional sales distribution  
- Highlight best-selling products  

---

## 📊 Key Metrics
- 💰 Total Sales: **2.26M**  
- 📦 Total Orders: **9800**  

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- Power BI  
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)  
- Data Modeling  

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features
- 📊 KPI cards for sales and order tracking  
- 📅 Monthly sales trend analysis  
- 🛍️ Sales by category (Technology, Furniture, Office Supplies)  
- 🌍 Sales distribution by region  
- 🏆 Top 5 products by sales  
- 🔍 Interactive filters (Region, Category, Order ID)

## 📊 Dashboard Breakdown

### 🔝 Top Section (KPIs)
- Displays **Total Sales (2.26M)** and **Total Orders (9800)**  
- Provides a quick overview of business performance  

---

### 📈 Middle Section

- **Sales Over Time**
  - Monthly trend showing fluctuations and growth  
  - Peak sales observed towards the end of the year  

- **Sales by Category**
  - Technology generates the highest revenue  
  - Furniture and Office Supplies follow  

---

### 📉 Bottom Section

- **Sales by Region**
  - West region leads in sales  
  - East and Central show strong performance  
  - South region contributes comparatively less  

- **Top 5 Products**
  - High-value products contribute significantly to revenue  
  - Example: Canon imageCLASS series leads  

- **Filters**
  - Region filter  
  - Category filter  
  - Order ID filter  

---

## 💡 Key Insights
- 📈 Sales peak during later months, indicating seasonal demand  
- 💻 Technology category generates the highest revenue  
- 🌍 West region shows consistent and strong performance  
- 🏆 A few top products contribute a large share of total sales
